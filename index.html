About Me

Hello! I'm Grzegorz, a Security Engineer based in Kraków, Poland, with expertise in cloud security, security operations, and security consulting. I enjoy building secure, reliable systems that process millions of security events.

At Zendesk, I manage threat prevention and detection tools, oversee SIEM platforms, and build high-throughput, reliable log ingestion pipelines.
